BANANA OIL :)

e-mail: bananaoilband@gmail.com

"Jazz Fusion Supergroup" from Glasgow, featuring Joe Howe (Tenor Sax, Keys), Niall Morris (Electric Bass) and Laurie Pitt (Drums).

Formed in 2017, the group is based around systematic, playful and inventive improvisation, with all members contributing ideas equally to the whole. Regular live shows have featured parades, hessian sacks, wet look gel, and medievalist films about public shaming.

Influenced equally by post punk, jazz skronk, rigorous funk & vivid prog, Banana Oil take a playful and always-DIY approach to playing music. The group has performed a number of "traditional" Scottish ceilidhs, collaborated with artists for performances, and perform as the "smooth jazz" house band at Civic House Kitchen
